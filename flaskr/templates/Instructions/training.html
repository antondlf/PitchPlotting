Training

Now for the training! For each sentence, you will imitate a native speaker and compare your recording to theirs. Then you will get to try again with the same sentence, applying what you have learned, before repeating the process with the question version of the sentence. Remember we are focusing on intonation — basically the melody of speech — so don’t worry if you mispronounce a word.
