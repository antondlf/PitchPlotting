This study is aimed at understanding how people learn intonation in a second language. You will be asked to complete two training sessions and a follow up. Each session will be conducted in this website.

Things you'll need:

  • A quiet space to work in.
  • A microphone (if you have a quality mic that is best).
  • An internet connection

For each session, you will log in to the website with the username and password that have been provided to you. This will take you to a home page listing all of the sessions, click on the appropriate section for that day.
