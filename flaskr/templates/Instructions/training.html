Training

Well done! Now for the training.
In this section you will imitate a native speaker and compare your intonation with hers.
You’ll try each sentence two times so you can apply what you learn. Then you’ll do the same sentence as a question.
As before, you can click the buttons or press the spacebar to advance through them in order.

Remember to wait for the record button to turn green before starting to speak.
