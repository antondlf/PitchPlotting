Test Recordings

In this section you will read Italian statements and questions out loud.

Click 'Start' to start recording. Your browser will probably ask you if you want to allow the website to use your microphone. Once you allow this the recorder will initialize. This can take a few seconds, so a “Wait…” message will appear under the controls.

The recording won’t start until this message turns to “Go!”. While recording, try to make sure you, or anyone around you don't make any noise since it could interfere with the processing.

Once you have recorded your voice, you can press the “Stop” button, which will stop the recorder and store the audio on the server. This button should also make the “Next Activity” button available, which will take you to the next sentence.
