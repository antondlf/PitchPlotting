Learning Italian Intonation

This study is aimed at understanding how people learn intonation in a second language. You will be asked to complete two training sessions and a follow up. Each session will be conducted in this website.

Things you'll need:

  • A quiet space to work in.
  • A microphone (if you have a quality mic that is best).
  • An internet connection

For each session, you will log in to the website with the username and password that have been provided to you. Each session consists of three parts, first you will make recordings of you reading a few sentences outloud, then you will do a training, and finally you will record some more sentences at the end.

If you run into any issues, contact cadelafuente@ucsb.edu for support.
