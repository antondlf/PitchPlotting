Learning Italian Intonation

Welcome! In this study, we are testing a training for intonation (the melody of speech) in Italian, distinguishing statements and questions. You will start by reading a few sentences out loud. Then you will do the training, where you get to compare your intonation to a native speaker of Italian, and you will finish by reading a few more sentences out loud.
