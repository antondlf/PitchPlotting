Training

In this section, you will listen to a native speaker of Italian and try to match their intonation. Press the “Play Recording” to listen to a recording of a native speaker pronouncing the sentence.

{% if condition == 'a' %}

Press “Record”, wait for the “Go!” sign, and try to imitate the native speaker’s production. Once you press “Stop”, you will be redirected to another page (this may take a few seconds to process).

This page will display a plot of your intonation against the native speaker, so you can compare both traces and see what you did right and what you did wrong. The example here is from a learner who hasn't quite hit the right intonation pattern but is almost there. You can see that the second syllable has quite a different intonation when compared to the native speaker, but the rest of the sentence aligns. You can evaluate your own intonation visually like this, and you also have the opportunity to listen to yourself by pressing "Listen to yourself". Try to order get a better idea of where you went wrong by listening to your recording and comparing the pitch traces.
